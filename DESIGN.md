# DESIGN.md — Borges Assessoria Imobiliária

> Luxury real estate brand. 30 years of expertise. Premium feel, clean and trustworthy.

## 1. Visual Theme

Dark hero, white body, warm gold accents. Sophisticated without being cold.

- **Style**: luxury editorial meets professional trust
- **Mood**: premium, warm, authoritative

## 2. Colors

- **Gold (accent/CTA)**: `#C4933A`
- **Dark (hero/footer)**: `#1C0F07`
- **Background**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Surface soft**: `#F8F5F0`
- **Text primary**: `#1a1a1a`
- **Text muted**: `#6B6B6B`
- **Border**: `#E5E0D8`

Gold for CTA buttons, links, highlights. Dark for hero overlay and footer. Surface soft for alternating sections.

## 3. Typography

- **Display/Headings**: `DM Serif Display` (Google Fonts, serif) — elegant, editorial
- **Body**: `Inter` (Google Fonts, sans-serif) — readable, modern
- **Scale**: 12/14/16/18/20/24/32/40/56/72

Hero headline: 56–72px, DM Serif Display, white.
Section headings: 32–40px, DM Serif Display, dark.
Body: 16–18px, Inter.

## 4. Spacing

8pt baseline grid. Section padding: 80–120px vertical. Card padding: 24px.

## 5. Components

- **CTA Primary**: gold bg `#C4933A`, white text, no border-radius (or 2px max), uppercase tracking-wide
- **CTA Secondary**: transparent, white border, white text (on dark) / dark border (on light)
- **Cards**: white, subtle shadow, 1px border `#E5E0D8`, hover lift
- **Nav**: transparent on hero, white with shadow on scroll

## 6. Motion

150–250ms ease transitions. Hover lift on cards (translateY -4px). Fade-in sections on scroll.

## 7. Anti-patterns

- No bright/saturated colors other than gold
- No rounded corners above 4px on primary elements
- No playful fonts or informal tone
- No clutter — whitespace is premium
